About Jim

James "Jim" Yergey retired from his profession as a chemist for Merck in 2014 and chased a dream of opening a brewery. Yergey Brewing opening in 2016, originally on 518 Bank Street in Emmaus, Pennsylvania. Yergey Brewing shared the building with Triple Sun
