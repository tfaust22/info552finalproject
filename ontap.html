Is This Just Fantasy – Bohemian-Style Pilsner

4.9% ABV

This golden lager has a rich bready aroma with subtle grassy and herbal notes from the Czech Saaz hops. On the palate the heirloom pilsner malt’s lovely biscuity, cereal notes provide background to the spicy, herbal hop bitterness. The finish is dry with a mild herbal bitterness that will have you asking “Is this the real life?”

Otter Rock IPA – American IPA

7.0% ABV

This is a hop forward, West Coast IPA, with big grapefruit nose and tons of citrus hoppy goodness on the palette. The malt is there to keep things in check, but the hops shine through to the dry, slightly bitter finish.

57th Edition Session IPA – Session IPA

4.7% ABV

This pale straw session IPA has wonderful fruity aromas of pear and tangerine, and a subtle vinous, white wine character. The flavors are reminiscent of mandarin oranges, and the finish is just slightly bitter. With the fruitiness and the low alcohol this is definitely a sessionable beer! This 57th edition of our Session IPA Series features exclusively Nelson Sauvin hops.

Who Runs The Wort – American Pale Ale

5.3% ABV

This light golden pale ale has wonderful tropical and stone fruit aromas invite you in. Upon tasting, those notes blend into it’s zippy herbal and lemon hop profile supported by a light malt body, and clean, crisp, and just slightly bitter finish.

Left-Handed Monkey Wrench – Belgian-Style Tripel Ale

10.8% ABV

Aroma of subtle banana and fresh apple esters mingles with a light maltiness, while the flavor blends the slightly spicy yeast phenols and soft sweetness from the Belgian Pilsner malt. The finish is dry with a warming alcohol note.

How Sweet It Is – NITRO Milk Stout

4.9% ABV

A firm, tan nitro head develops, releasing aromas of dark malt, coffee and chocolate. This velvety stout provides a rich, smooth mouthfeel with an underlying sweetness of lactose and roasty notes that linger at the end.

My Lightning Too – Belgian Style Dubbel

7.7% ABV

This deep copper ale has a rich fruity nose of banana and pear with a subtle caramel malt sweetness. Taste reveals full-bodied malt flavors of caramel and dark fruit complemented by delicate spicy phenols and a hint of earthy noble hop character.

K.I.S.S. – Scottish – Style Ale

5.5% ABV

The aroma of our caramel colored Keep It Simple Scottish-style ale is reminiscent of lady finger cookies with a slight apple fruit note. On the palate the mild toasted bread and caramel flavors meld with the fruity esters to produce an easy drinking beer that finished medium-dry with grainy malt notes.

Saúde, Feijoa – Catherina Sour with Feijoa

6.0% ABV

Catharina sours, Brazilian in origin, are light wheat ales with a clean lactic sour note designed to highlight the fruit of that region. This batch features feijoa fruit puree, often called Brazilian guava, which provides its unique flavor of guava, strawberry and subtle eucalyptus, and is effervescent and refreshingly sour, Saúde!
