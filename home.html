What Are We Up To?

Recently, we teamed up with Habitat for Humanity to help build homes for families in need in the area. Habitat has helped thousands in the valley, and continues to help through the acts of volunteering. It was an honor to join their volunteers and give back to the community. We can't wait to help again! If you haven't signed up to volunteer, we encourage you to do so!
